lets talk more about the story. Rare (the creator of the game) partnered
with Disney to bring Jack Sparrow from Disney's pirates of the caribean
to Sea of Thieves in a thrilling campaign. I would highly recomend it
as it is possibly the best campaign I have ever played.


Chapter one of the campaign is it bit slow, but a great beggining
to a great campaign. Chapter two was extremely fun and was one of
the most fun things I have ever done on an xbox. Chapter three was...
fun? It was interesting, but a bit basic compared to the last two chapters.
I haven't played chapters 4 or 5 yet, but I definitely have some high hopes.


*Spoilers ahead. Read at your own risk* You start chapter one by going to where the castaway is on any outpost,
and voting for the voyage called "A Pirates Life". The castaway will
begin to start talking and giving story. Once she is done talking, a portal
will open and you need to sail through it. You are taken to
the sea of the damned where you see spirits in small row boats.
You go through another portal and are introduced to a rather spooky island.
You go onto the island and find a cave with a statue in front of it.
Inside the cave is a series of puzzles that usually consist of lighting the lanters
with your lantern. After getting through the puzzles, you go outside and
are introduced to a huge dead shipyard, a lighthouse, and an abundace of eerieness.
After doing more puzzles to free the skull of a captain who helps you get into
and summon the ferry of the damned. Once summoned, you sneak onto it and free
Jack Sparrow. While you free him, the ship gets moving and gets attacked
by Davy Jones and his ginormous ship. After fending him and other enemies off
for a little while, the chapter ends with Jack Sparrow falling off the ferry and
you returning to the living world.

The next chapters continue the story with more puzzles and an abundance of fun.
Chapter two is definitely the best from what I have played, but each chapter has its own
charm. I would highly recommend this game, but there is more to it on the next page.
