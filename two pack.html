Lets talk about the rest of the game. While the campaign is truly amazing,
there is a lot more to Sea of Thieves than in-depth stories.
The main mode that everyone plays is the adventure mode. In adventure mode, you
are a pirate trying to collect money by doing tasks. What kinds of tasks? Well to answer
that, we must talk about the trading groups/emissaries.

There are a total of 5 trading groups that will give you quests.
Each trading company can be leveled up by doing quests for them. Once you reach
a certain level with that trading company, you can buy an emissary flag from them.
Once you buy a flag, it can be voted on to raise. Once it is raised, you become an emissary
for that trading company and get lots of benefits. Now lets break down the individual trading companies.


    THE GOLD HOARDERS
    is a group of hoarders that give you chest finding missions that are relativly easy. They are
    the first group that you will be doing missions for, and can be done with either a group
    or by yourself. Definitely a staple in the Sea Of Thieves.


THE ORDER OF SOULS
is a group of crazy people who give you skeleton killing missions that can be a bit hard. They a typically
the second group that you will be doing missions for, and are usually done alone. They to are a staple in the
Sea Of Thieves



THE MERCHANT ALLIANCE
is a group of buisness oriented people that give you cargo finding and delivering missions. They are
the third group that you will do. They are the easiest and can be easily done alone. While not known extremely
well, they are very useful.



THE REAPERS BONES
is a group unlike any other. While most other groups can be found at outposts, the reapers are at only one
location. They also will buy anything that you have, including the reaper's chest and humble gifts.
They even buy emmisary flags. They are a very interesting group in the Sea Of Thieves.



There are a few more trading groups, but these are the main ones. I highly
recommend that you buy Sea Of Thieves due to the vast amount of things to do in it.
